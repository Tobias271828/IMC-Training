%Problem hier
Suppose that \( v_1, v_2, \dots, v_d \) are unit vectors in \( \mathbb{R}^d \). Prove that there exists a unit vector \( u \) such that
\[
|u \cdot v_i| \leq \frac{1}{\sqrt{d}}
\]
for \( i = 1, 2, \dots, d \).\\
\;\\
(Here \( \cdot \) denotes the usual scalar product on \( \mathbb{R}^d \).)
