Assume that an $n\times n$ matrix $A$ with real entries satisfies $||A^{k}-A^{k-1}||\le\frac{1}{2002k}$ for all positive integers $k$.\newline Prove that $||A^{k}||\le2002$ for all positive integers $k$.
