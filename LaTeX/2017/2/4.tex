f_1=1;\;f_{n+1}'=f_nf_{n+1}\;\text{on $(0,1)$},\;\text{and}\;f_{n+1}(0)=1.
$$Show that $\lim\limits_{n\to \infty}f_n(x)$ exists for every $x\in [0,1)$ and determine the limit function.
